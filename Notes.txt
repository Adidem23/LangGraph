Autonomy is the most promising feature of the ai agentic system but we have to control autonomy of an ai agentic system 

We can restrict autonomy of an agentic system by some of the ways which are discussed below : 
- Premission Scope
- Human in the Loop
- override controls 
- Guardrails / Policies

- Memory
